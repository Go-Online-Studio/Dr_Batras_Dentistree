About The Treatment

What is Invisalign?

Invisalign uses custom-made clear aligners to gradually move teeth into proper alignment. These aligners are removable, comfortable, and nearly invisible, making them a popular alternative to traditional braces.

At Dr. Batra's Dentistree, Invisalign treatment is planned digitally using advanced smile scanning and customized aligner planning for accurate and comfortable smile correction.
